About Me

I've been around for tewnty something years, and in that time I've picked up a few hobbies. I enjoy going to the movies, fishing, playing board games, and a bunch of other things that would make this list too long.

I like to learn by doing. This means spending time fixing things that break, trying new activities, and making things (be they creative, practical, or both).

From August 2015 to May 2019 I attended Cornell University where I studied Electrical and Computer Engineering. I worked hard in my studies, and graduated with a Bachelors of Science. Though I do consider this an achievement, a degree is just a piece of paper. My true takeaways from Cornell are my experiences, be they hours in a lab trying to get my project to work correctly or a Saturday night playing games with friends.
